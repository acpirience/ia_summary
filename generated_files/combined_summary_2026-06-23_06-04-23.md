## Deepening AI Understanding: Capabilities, Limitations, and Practical Application

A comprehensive suite of educational resources, including new courses, videos, and practical guides, has been released to enhance user understanding of Artificial Intelligence (AI) models. The primary goal is to empower users with a deep understanding of AI's strengths and limitations, fostering greater confidence in its application.

A new, free, and product-agnostic course focuses specifically on the core properties, capabilities, and inherent limitations of AI technology. This resource is designed as a direct follow-up to the existing "AI Fluency and Foundations" course, aiming to provide learners with essential knowledge for effective and responsible AI engagement.
