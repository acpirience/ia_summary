Large Language Models (LLMs) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) are rapidly advancing, with a strong focus on increasing autonomy and efficiency. This progress is evident across various domains, from sophisticated problem-solving to practical applications in business and cybersecurity. However, these advancements also bring challenges related to security, cost management, and ethical considerations.
