## AI Accuracy and Corporate Liability

The increasing deployment of AI at scale has brought critical attention to its accuracy and the question of legal liability for AI-generated content.

*   **Google's AI Overviews Liability:** A landmark German regional court ruling in Munich found Google directly liable for false claims made by its AI-generated Overviews. The court issued a temporary injunction preventing Google from spreading false information about two Munich-based publishers. Google's argument that users understand AI outputs are not always correct and should be verified was rejected; the court determined that AI Overviews generate original content, making Google responsible as the sole owner of its models.
