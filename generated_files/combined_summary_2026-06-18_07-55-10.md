### Key Developments in AI and Coding

*   **GLM-5.2 Model by Z.ai (High Priority - multiple mentions across documents):**
    *   **Capabilities:** An open-weights model (MIT licensed) designed for advanced coding and agentic tasks. It boasts a 1 million-token context window, a 5x increase from its predecessor, allowing it to process entire codebases for refactoring, debugging, or extension.
    *   **Performance:** Achieved a benchmark score of 81.0 on Terminal-Bench 2.1 (up from 62.0 in GLM-5.1) and is competitive with, or even surpasses, proprietary models like GPT 5.5 on coding, reasoning, and math benchmarks. Some reports state it's just below Claude Opus 4.8.
    *   **Cost-Efficiency:** Priced at $1.40 per million input tokens, roughly one-sixth of GPT-5.5.
    *   **Availability:** Available on Hugging Face, Z.ai API, and hosted on Ollama's cloud in the US on NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs, offering zero data retention.
    *   **Integrations:** Can be launched with popular tools like Claude Code, Codex App, Hermes Agent, and OpenClaw.
